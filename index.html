Hi, we are Linda and Istvan Takacs, and we are so happy that you found us!

We are a husband and wife duo who live the life of art. We specialize in Studio Newborn and Baby's First Year Photography. We are located in McKinney TX and we serve McKinney, Plano, Prosper, Celina, Little Elm and surrounding areas. Our goal is to create award winning images of you and your loved ones, for you to have and display proudly in your home for years to come. To us its not just about capturing moments, its also about CREATING beautiful memories that will last a lifetime and give you the warm fuzzies all over again every time that you look at them. Those little details: the pouty lips, the tiny fingers, the itty bitty toes are priceless. We have won international competitions with our newborn images, competing against some of the biggest names in the industry . We believe that simplicity is elegance. Our work is focused on the subject, the feelings and not so much on props and distractions. We look forward to meeting you and your loved ones and being part of this amazing journey with you!
